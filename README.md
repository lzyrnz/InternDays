InternDays
InternDays is a personal web application built to streamline the tracking of On-the-Job Training (OJT) hours and serve as a digital journal. Keeping track of daily tasks, total hours worked, and key takeaways during an internship can be difficult, so this platform centralizes everything into a practical time-tracker and professional diary.

The core of the application handles time tracking by logging daily check-ins and check-outs. Alongside this, a daily journal provides a space to document experiences, completed tasks, and newly acquired skills. To keep the timeline accurate, the system also accounts for scheduled holidays and days off.

As you log your shifts, the dashboard calculates your progress—displaying it as a dynamic percentage that updates directly based on your input data. It actively compares your total hours logged against your final OJT goal, and uses that information to calculate your estimated finish date based on your current pace.
